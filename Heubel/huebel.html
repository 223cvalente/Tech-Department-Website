Automotive Classes

Automotive 1

Automotive 1 is an introductory class which will teach students about small engines, engine theory, the basic automobile and simple car care. Almost everyone drives a car so knowing about how they work being able to do simple repairs is a very useful life skill.

Automotive 2

A follow up to Automotive 1 which will allow students to delve deeper into learning about automobiles. Students will work together throughout the year on a large restoration project. They will take apart an automobile then learn about and restore its components before reassembling it to its former glory.

Honors Innovation and Design

This class acts as the third year of tech classes. For automotive, this class is much more independent than the previous two, truly giving students the opportunity to govern themselves. They will help with running the level 2 students restoration project as well as being given their own problems to solve using all of the knowledge they have gained from the previous two classes.
