Engineering Graphics Classes

Engineering Graphics

In Engineering Graphics students learn how to rapidly prototype designs to teach the fundamentals of drafting. They will learn the basics of 3D modeling, design prototyping, and problem solving skills to aid them in the future. Students will also be given the oppurtunity to produce models using 3D printers, CNC routers, and laser cutters.



Honors Engineering Graphics

In Honors Engineering Graphics students are given different problems and are then tasked with solving them using their prior knowledge to develop a solution.
