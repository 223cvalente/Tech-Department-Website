Honors Innovation and Design

An intro to the design process for IB Students

Students from all over the tech department who have an interest in pursuing their technology related interests further gather in this class to work on projects that will deepen their understanding of technology. Students in this class will participate in dynamic, collaborative activities that will combine elements of each of their interests. This class will also teach students to bend to the constraints of a real world situation and find ways to be more efficient. The projects done in this class are all pertinent to the 21st century and can be utilized in a portfolio.
