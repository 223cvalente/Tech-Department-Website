IB Design Tech

An intro to the design process for IB Students

IB Design Tech 1 is focused on analysis, design development, synthesis, and evaluation. Students will learn design literacy by developing critical thinking and design skills, which can be applied in a variety of frameworks. The class is focused around the design process to give students the basic understanding that they will apply in IB Design Tech 2.
IB Design Tech 2 provides students with the means to analyze problems and develop and test solutions. Students will further develop their critical-thinking and design skills by applying them to practical contexts. Students will be tasked with hands-on projects and experiences to test their skills and prepare them for the IB Internal Assessment.
