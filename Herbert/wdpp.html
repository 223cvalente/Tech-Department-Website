Web Design Pathway Program

Web Design 1

A rudimentary and introductory start to creating a website and the design process as a whole. It teaches students to think like designers and utilize foundational HTML and CSS to create websites that they will build upon over time. Students will also learn how to utilize frameworks such as Bootstrap that will hasten their workflow and make them more efficient web designers.

Web Design 2

Students will apply their prior web design knowledge to develop problem solving skills and create interactive websites with JavaScript. Students will be expected to make more advanced and creative websites as their skill portfolio grows. Though they will not get real clients yet, they will apply their new skills to create more professional websites for mock clients.

Web Design 3

Students will engage in the highest level of web development and are expected to create efficient, functional websites for everyday use. They will engage in work-based learning projects, where they are assigned real clients to create tailor-made professional websites for. Students are also given the opportunity to take the NOCTI Exam to earn credentials. Passing this class with a C or higher will give students college credit at a significantly reduced cost.
